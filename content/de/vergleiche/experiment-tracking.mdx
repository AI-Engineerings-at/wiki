# Experiment Tracking — Vergleich

| Tool | Self-Hosted | Free Cloud | Pricing | UI | Best for |
|------|-------------|-----------|---------|----|---------  |
| **MLflow** | ✅ | ✅ | Free | Simple | Teams, Open-Source |
| **W&B** | ⚠️ | ✅ | Pay-as-you-go | ⭐⭐⭐ | ML Teams |
| **Neptune** | ⚠️ | ✅ | Flexible | ⭐⭐⭐ | Enterprises |
| **ClearML** | ✅ | ✅ | Free | ⭐⭐ | Automation |

## MLflow

Pros: Open-Source, kostenlos, einfach
Cons: UI weniger polished

```python
mlflow.log_param("model", "gpt-4")
mlflow.log_metric("accuracy", 0.95)
```

## Weights & Biases

Pros: Beste UI, Team-Features
Cons: Cloud-only (teuer)

## Neptune

Pros: Enterprise-ready, flexible
Cons: Komplexer Setup

## ClearML

Pros: Kostenlos mit Automation
Cons: Weniger Community

## Empfehlung

- **Open-Source Teams**: MLflow
- **ML-Focused Teams**: Weights & Biases
- **Enterprise**: Neptune
- **CI/CD Automation**: ClearML
